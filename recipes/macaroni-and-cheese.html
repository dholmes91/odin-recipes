Macaroni & Cheese

Description

Baked mac and cheese. It's nearly the universal comfort food, and there are SO many variations! Of course, there's no “right” way or “wrong” way, so I'm here sharing the way I like to make my mac and cheese

Ingredients

  • Elbow pasta
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil & drain pasta
  2. Combine cheese sauce
  3. Cook sauce
  4. Add cheese, combine with pasta
  5. Transfer to baking dish, add shredded cheese
  6. Bake & enjoy
