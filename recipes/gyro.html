Chicken Gyro

Description

This recipe for homemade Chicken Gyros boasts huge flavor with just a few simple steps. Made with a blend of spices with the perfect balance of herbs, smokiness, warmth, and aroma, gyro night can be enjoyed in the comfort of your own home! You'll be blown away with your very own creation.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thighs
  • Greek Yogurt
  • Lemon Juice
  • Red wine vinegar
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Spices
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Tzatziki sauce
  • Pitas

Steps

  1. Preheat grill for 5-10 minutes
  2. Grill thighs for 5-7 minutes per side
  3. Roughly chop thighs and assemble gyros
  4. Serve with olives or falafel
